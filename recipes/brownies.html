Chocolate Caramel Brownies

Description

Chewy Chocolate Brownies that are soft on the outside and gooey on the inside. They will make your mouth water! A great spin on a classic treat!

Easy to make and definitely easy to eat! Try making these brownies today!

Ingredients

  • 14 ounces caramels
  • ½ cup evaporated milk
  • 1 (18.25 ounces) package German chocolate cake mix
  • ⅓ cup evaporated milk
  • ¾ cup butter, melted
  • ¼ cup chopped pecans
  • 2 cups milk chocolate chips
